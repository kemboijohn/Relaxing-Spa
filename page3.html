Privacy Policy

Introduction: Relaxing Spa is committed to protecting the privacy of our customers. This Privacy Policy outlines the information we collect, how it is used, and the steps we take to protect it.

Information Collection: We collect information from customers when they book an appointment or sign up for our newsletter. This information may include but is not limited to: name, contact information, payment information, and health information relevant to spa services.

Use of Information: The information we collect is used to provide our customers with the best possible spa experience. This includes but is not limited to: booking appointments, processing payments, sending newsletters and promotional materials, and improving our services.

Sharing of Information: Relaxing Spa will not sell or share customer information with third parties except as required by law or for the purpose of providing our services.

Data Security: We take measures to secure customer information, including but not limited to: secure servers, encryption of sensitive information, and restricted access to information by authorized personnel only.

Access to Information: Customers have the right to access their personal information and request correction or deletion of inaccurate information.

Changes to Privacy Policy: Relaxing Spa reserves the right to modify this Privacy Policy at any time without notice. Your continued use of the website and services indicates your acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.

By using the Relaxing Spa website and services, you agree to be bound by this Privacy Policy. If you have any questions, please contact us.
